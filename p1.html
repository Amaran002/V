The film stars Sivakarthikeyan and Aditi Shankar, while Saritha, Mysskin, Yogi Babu and Sunil play supporting roles. It revolves around a comic book artist who is forced to take on a corrupt politician when a voice he hears in his mind foretells events and puts him in precarious situations. Maaveeran was announced in July 2022. Principal photography began the following August and wrapped in June 2023. The film was released on 14 July 2023 to positive reviews from critics, and became a commercial success.
